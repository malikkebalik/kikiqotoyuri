Days of Our Lives Spoilers: Jada Day-Drinks After 2 Sad Reminders Stephanie Helps Friend Let Loo

Days of Our Lives (DOOL) spoilers reveal that Jada Hunter (Elia Cantu) will have a pretty rough day thanks to a couple of sad reminders. For starters, there’ll be some bittersweet moments as Jada bumps into Eric Brady (Greg Vaughan) and congratulates him on Jude Brady’s adoption.

Although Jada will obviously be happy for Eric, this could also be a reminder that she almost had a baby with Eric herself.

Jada is at peace with her decision to terminate the pregnancy, but Eric’s baby news could still leave her thinking about the road not taken.

DOOL fans know Jada’s ex-husband has been on her mind lately, so she might find herself wondering about the children she could’ve had with Everett Lynch (Blake Berris) and the family they could’ve built together.

In the midst of all this, Days spoilers say Jada will face another difficult reminder of the past.

It’ll be the anniversary of her father’s demise, so Jada will certainly think about Marcus Hunter (Richard Biggs) and all the memories.

Since Jada no doubt misses her dad, that’ll be another tough blow to contend with.

Days spoilers say Jada will need some support, so Steve Johnson (Stephen Nichols) will reach out and reminisce about Marcus since they were good buddies.

However, the real comfort may come later since Jada will do some day-drinking with Stephanie Johnson (Abigail Klein) at the pub.

It’ll be a little early to be guzzling booze, but Stephanie and Jada will do it anyway.

Days spoilers say that’ll lead to some talk about their complicated exes, so Stephanie will help Jada let loose with alcohol as they both share a laugh.

Stephanie will hear some stories about Jada’s ex-hubby and push to see a pic of him, but Harris Michaels (Steve Burton) will throw off the big reveal when he walks in and gets Jada’s attention.

Days of Our Lives spoilers say Stephanie will have to find out about Jada and Everett’s former marriage some other time, so stay tuned for updates on the drama that’s brewing during the week of December 11-15 and beyond.
