Who is Perry Johnson?

Johnson is the founder of Perry Johnson Registrars Inc. in Troy, Michigan to perform audits and issue certifications ensuring that other companies’ quality management systems conform to international standards. Perry Johnson’s Campaign for Governor of Michigan Begins with a Super Bowl ad

Former Michigan gubernatorial candidate and Republican businessman Perry Johnson is forming a presidential exploratory committee and planning to air ads supporting his candidature in the crucial state of Iowa during the Super Bowl ad.

On Thursday, Johnson announced the establishment of his presidential committee paving the way for him to begin soliciting and spending donations. The next few months will see Johnson formally announce his candidature for president of the United States and begin a bus tour of Iowa. “I will be visiting Iowa in the next week,” Johnson said.
