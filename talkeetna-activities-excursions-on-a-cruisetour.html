Talkeetna Activities &amp; Excursions on a Cruisetour

Mt. Denali Flyer with Glacier Landing

Set off on an exciting flightseeing trip and enjoy bird’s eye views of Mt. Denali within six miles of the magnificent Kahiltna Glacier. Fly over the remote western end of Denali Park and experience amazing natural beauty at a fantastic vantage point along the way. Enjoy incredible views of Mt. Denali, Mt. Foraker, and Mt. Hunter above the longest glacier in Denali National Park. Continue to the climber’s base camp at 7,200 feet. This experience also includes a glacier landing. 

Cost: $537 per person 
Duration: 1.75 hrs.

Available with Cruisetours:  1A, 1B, 3A, 3B, 5A, 5B, 7A, 7B, 8A, 8B, & 9A
