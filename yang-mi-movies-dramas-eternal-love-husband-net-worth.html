The career of Yang Mi- Movies and Dramas

Before she even knew what acting was, Yang Mi made her debut at the age of four where she appeared in a historical drama, Tang Ming Huang.

Slowly after that Mi began appearing in other minor roles such as King of Beggars(1992) and The Story of a Noble Family. 

Likewise, in 2002, the young actress signed a contract with Li Shaohong’s agency, Rosat Entertainment. With that being said, Yang Mi rose to fame in the late 2000s.

Her breakthrough came from the role of Nie Xiaoqian in the shenmo drama series, Strange Tales of Liao Zhai in 2005. 

Also, for her performance in the drama, The Return of the Condor Heroes(2006), Yang Mi received her positive responses and was nominated for the Best Actress award at the China TV Golden Eagle Award. 

Then her role in the 2009 fantasy action drama, Chinese Paladin 3, elevated her fame even more. As years passed on, Mi kept honing her skills and started getting a lot of acclaimed roles in some major films and dramas.

Through the 2011 historical romance drama, Palace, she got mainstream success. On top of that, Yang has voted the Most Popular Actress at the 17th Shanghai Television Festival. 

Moreover, that same year, she starred in the horror film, Mysterious Island which raked in more than 70 million yuan at the box office. It is the most successful Chinese horror movie to date.
