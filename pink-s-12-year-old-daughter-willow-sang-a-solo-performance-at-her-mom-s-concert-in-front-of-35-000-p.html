On Thursday night, singer Pink had her 12-year-old daughter Willow Sage Hart join her on stage to give a solo performance of her mother's song "Cover Me In Sunshine."

Pink's husband of 12 years Carey Hart, 47, shared the footage on Instagram, saying, "So proud of Wills to get up on stage w/ mama @pink!!!!! Must be a surreal moment for mama to have w/ her lil girl on stage."

The 35,000 fans attending the concert in Bolton, England, were treated to the surprise performance, which allowed the young singer to show off her vocal range.

It's not the first time Pink, 43, has showcased her daughter's singing ability. In 2018, the two each sang a record on "The Greatest Showman: Reimagined" soundtrack. In December 2022, Pink shared a video of Willow's first singing recital on Instagram. The then 11-year-old performed "The Rose Song," originally sung by Olivia Rodrigo for "High School Musical: The Musical: The Series."

Willow currently has what Pink called a "minimum wage job," helping out on her mom's Summer Carnival Tour.

"I said it's about $22.50 a show depending how long I go, if I run over. She goes, 'I'll take $20. it's easier to do the math.' I'm like 'That's not how you negotiate for yourself.' I'm like, 'You'll take $25 so it's easier math," Pink told "Today" show hosts Savannah Guthrie and Carson Daly.
