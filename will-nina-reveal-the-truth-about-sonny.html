Will Nina Reveal the Truth About Sonny?

TV

This week's 'General Hospital' spoilers suggest that Nina has had enough of keeping secrets and lies — and she just might reveal the truth about Sonny.

Published on August 5, 2021

2 min read

For once, the latest General Hospital spoilers will focus on something other than Peter’s body. (It’s good to switch things up once in a while, right?) This week, the show will focus on other people carrying secrets and lies around. Nina, as we know, has her fair share of drama. But will this be the week that she finally comes clean about Sonny?

[Spoiler alert: Potential spoilers for upcoming episodes of General Hospital are up ahead.]

‘General Hospital’ spoilers reveal Nina’s struggle with the truth about Sonny

According to Soaps @ SheKnows, the latest General Hospital spoilers reveal that Nina’s cracking under the weight of keeping all these secrets. And, she just might spill what she knows about the not-really-dead Sonny.

The outlet has a rundown of the teasers provided by the network. The first teaser came from Tuesday’s episode, where we know that Nina started to get in her feelings after her little chat with Wiley. She was going back and forth with spilling the beans on the secrets she’s been holding. But, ultimately, she held firm.

Next week, expect Nina and “Mike” to get even closer than they were before. How far they’ll go, though, remains to be seen.

Brook Lynn may still prove to be a problem for Nina

But Nina isn’t just holding onto Sonny’s (Mike’s) secrets. Previous General Hospital spoilers revealed that she’s holding on to Brook Lynn’s secrets, too.

We know that Brook Lynn has been giving poor Valentin the what-for about Maxie’s baby, which she’s claiming is hers. Valentin has his own problems, though, about poor Peter and his role in handing him over to Cesar.

But other people are starting to catch on that Brook Lynn is hiding something. It remains to be seen who will ultimately drop the hammer — and when. The probable fallout will definitely be one to watch in the coming weeks.

Meanwhile, elsewhere in Port Charles

SoapHub has a round-up of what else we can expect to see in Port Charles this week.

Their General Hospital spoilers reveal that Curtis will be sick to his stomach when Stella is brought to the hospital. He’ll be thankful to Cameron for taking care of her, to be sure. But what’s really causing Stella to feel this way — guilt, or something worse?

And speaking of Brook Lynn, Sasha may tell her that she has a “perk” in mind for her — though what it is, and what it entails, remains to be seen in upcoming episodes.
