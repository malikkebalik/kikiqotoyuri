Is Greg Olsen Gay? The Truth Behind the Rumors

Gregory Walter Olsen, born on March 11, 1985, is an American football commentator and used to play as a tight end in the NFL for 14 seasons. He started in college with the Miami Hurricanes and got drafted by the Chicago Bears in 2007.

Olsen also played for the Carolina Panthers, making three Pro Bowls. He made NFL history by being the first tight end to have three consecutive seasons with over 1,000 receiving yards. His last season was with the Seattle Seahawks in 2020.

But beyond his football achievements, there have been rumors and discussions about Olsen’s personal life in the online world. This article delves into the topic, exploring the facts and addressing the rumors surrounding Greg Olsen’s s*xuality.
