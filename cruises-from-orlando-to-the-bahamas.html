Cruises From Orlando to The Bahamas

Make your getaway to the balmy tropics on a cruise from Orlando to The Bahamas. Shop and dine in historic Nassau, and soak up the sunshine on the white sand beaches of Bimini. Some itineraries call at our award-winning private island, CocoCay, a paradise of sandy shores, thrilling waterslides, a shimmering lagoon pool, and exclusive beachfront cabanas.

Cruises from Orlando to The Bahamas can take you further afield, too, visiting Dutch St. Maarten and colorful Puerto Rico, or spending a day at arty, bohemian Key West. On board the ship, sunbathe by the sparkling pool and enjoy an al fresco lunch as your ship makes its way from one port to the next. In the evening, head to the Sunset Bar for cocktails to raise a glass to another perfect day. Whichever itinerary you choose, a cruise from Orlando with Celebrity Cruises will leave you renewed and refreshed.
