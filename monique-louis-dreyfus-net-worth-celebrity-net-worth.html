What is Monique Louis-Dreyfus' Net Worth?

Monique Louis-Dreyfus is a French businesswoman who has a net worth of $1.7 billion. Monique Louis-Dreyfus, also known as Monique Roosmale Nepveu, is a member of the Louis-Dreyfus family of entrepreneurs which established the Louis Dreyfus Group in 1851. Like most the family members, Monique derives her fortune from the commodities giant Louis Dreyfus Holdings, in which she holds a 12% stake. It was her brother, Robert Louis-Dreyfus, who ran the firm until 2009 when he died of leukemia.

Now, her sister-in-law, Margarita, chairs the firm. Margarita and Robert's two sisters are reportedly not on good terms since Robert's death. Also, while Margarita wants to buy the shares belonging to Monique and Robert's elder sister, Marie-Jeanne Meyer; the two sisters have not revealed their plans regarding their company's stakes. Seinfeld actress Julia Louis-Dreyfus is Monique's first cousin.
