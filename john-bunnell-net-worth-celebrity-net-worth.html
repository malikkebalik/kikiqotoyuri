What is John Bunnell's Net Worth?

John Bunnell is an American sheriff and television personality who has a net worth of $3 million. John Bunnell was born in Pendleton, Oregon in May 1944. He worked as the Sheriff of Multnomah County, Oregon from 1969 to 1995. Bunnell presented the television series World's Wildest Police Videos from 1998 to 2002 and he was also a co-executive producer for the show.

He also appeared in episodes of the TV series Cops, and American Detective. As an actor Bunnell starred in the movies Ghost World and Bad Santa. He also played himself in two episodes of the TV series Family Guy in 2009.
