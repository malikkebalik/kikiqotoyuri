The Bold and the Beautiful Spoilers: Jacqueline MacInnes Wood Announces Birth of Baby Boy Reveal

The Bold and the Beautiful (B&B) spoilers reveal that Jacqueline MacInnes Wood (Steffy Forrester) recently announced some exciting news on her Instagram account. B&B fans have been waiting to hear about the birth of her latest baby, so Wood finally posted a photo and admitted her family addition has arrived.

It turns out Wood had another son on August 27, so she’s raising four boys with her husband, Elan Ruspoli. The two of them are already proud parents to Rise, Lenix, and Brando, but now the boys have a new little brother.

“August 27th our baby boy was born,” Jacqueline MacInnes Wood said in her Instagram announcement. “Our hearts are so full. Thank you for all the messages. Just been taking this time to slow down and soak in this moment. #gratefulheart”

Of course, Wood is currently on her maternity leave, but she filmed a few more B&B episodes that’ll play out in the coming days.

The last airdate for Wood’s current stint will be September 7, which is when Steffy will decide she’s done dealing with Sheila Carter’s (Kimberlin Brown) drama.

After another showdown with Sheila, Steffy will pack up Kelly Spencer (Sophia Paras McKinlay) and Hayes Forrester Finnegan (Alexander and Chase Banks) for a trip to Europe.

Although John “Finn” Finnegan (Tanner Novlan) will be heartbroken over the separation, Steffy will feel like she has no choice but to head overseas and create some sort of Sheila barrier.

That’ll leave things up in the air when it comes to Steffy and Finn’s marriage, so Liam Spencer (Scott Clifton) will no doubt hope he can win Steffy’s heart back sooner or later.

Jacqueline MacInnes Wood will return down the road, but we wish her the best of luck with her new baby in the meantime! Wood has a gorgeous family and deserves this chance to enjoy some private bonding.
