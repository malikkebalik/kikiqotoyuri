What is Alex Goot's Net Worth?

Alex Goot is an American singer-songwriter and musician who has a net worth of $1.8 million. Alex Goot was born in Poughkeepsie, New York in March 1988. He gained his popularity through his YouTube channel GootMusic which has over a million subscribers. In addition to singing, he plays acoustic guitar, bass guitar, drums, and piano among others. Goot started recording music at 16 years old and used his own website along with various social media sites to gain notoriety.

He self-produces all of his audio and video in his own home. He released his debut studio album In Your Atmosphere which was funded through a KickStarter campaign. Goot's YouTube channel has almost two million subscribers and has been viewed more than 330 million times. In 2011 he won an MTV O Music Award for Best Fan Cover for "Hold It Against Me" by Britney Spears. Goot was also nominated for a Streamy Award in 2013 for Best Original Song for "Lightning".
