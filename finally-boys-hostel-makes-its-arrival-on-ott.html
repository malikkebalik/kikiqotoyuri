Finally, Boys Hostel makes its arrival on OTT

Finally, Boys Hostel makes its arrival on OTT

Published on Nov 10, 2023 9:00 am IST

Recent Kannada superhit, Hostel Hudugaru Bekagiddare, has been dubbed into Telugu as Boys Hostel and saw its cinema release on August 26, 2023. Directed by debutant Nithin Krishnamurthy, the film received mixed response from Telugu audiences.

After a substantial wait, the movie has made its OTT debut on ETV Win today. Those who missed the black comedy drama in theaters can now enjoy it on the digital streaming service. The Kannada version is already available on the ZEE5 platform.

Featuring Prajwal BP, Manjunath Nayaka, Rakesh Rajkumar, Srivatsa, and Tejas Jayanna Urs in significant roles, the film also includes Tharun Bhascker and Rashmi in cameo roles.

Presented by Rakshit Shetty under Paramvah Pictures, the movie boasts music composed by Ajaneesh Loknath and is a collaborative effort between Gulmohur Films and Varrun Studios. Stay tuned for more OTT updates.
