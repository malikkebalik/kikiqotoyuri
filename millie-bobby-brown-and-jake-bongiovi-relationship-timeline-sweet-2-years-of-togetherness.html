Millie Bobby Brown and Jake Bongiovi Relationship Timeline: Sweet 2 Years of Togetherness

Teen romance in the movies. Since making their relationship public, Millie Bobby Brown and Jake Bongiovi (real name: Jacob Hurley Bongiovi) have become regular fixtures on each other’s social media sites and on the red carpets of award shows.

Jon Bon Jovi’s son sparked dating rumors after captioning an Instagram photo of himself and the Stranger Things actress with the words “Bff 3” in June of 2021.

Their romance swiftly blossomed as they documented their shared Instagram moments, such as their holiday and Brown’s 18th birthday celebrations.

Bongiovi, one of the four children the “It’s My Life” singer had with Dorothea Hurley, captioned a February 2022 image from the birthday celebration in which she and her boyfriend dressed as Barbie and Ken with the heart emoji.

The former Enola Holmes student said “endless love” to her boyfriend.

They made their red carpet debut a month later, but have said little publicly about their budding romance. Brown let slip, though, that they don’t share many of the same passions.

Seeing her videos and listening to her music is a blast for me. In January 2022, the founder of Florence by Mills told Seventeen magazine, “I don’t really involve myself because I know that she’s simply too clever.” She went on to say that Rodrigo’s SOUR vinyl album was one of her Christmas presents that year.

1 Step Forward, 3 Steps Back’ is my favorite adage. Every time I hear it, it makes me want to cry. My partner threatened to murder me if I listened to that song again in his presence.

On the red carpet at the 2022 BAFTAs in March, Brown and Bongiovi flaunted their romance as she presented the Best Supporting Actor award to CODA superstar Troy Kotsur. Brown looked stunning in a black Louis Vuitton gown, while her partner wore a sharp Fendi suit.

The Godzilla vs. Kong starlet posted pictures from the couple’s red carpet date night to Instagram at the time, along with a string of heart emojis to express her feelings.

Brown had been linked to musician Jacob Sartorius prior to their romance, who is also a graduate of Pennington School. In January 2018, Us Weekly exclusively reported that the then-13-year-old actress was very close to the “Chapstick” singer. After seven months together, the romance between them died out.

“The choice between Jacob and I was fully mutual,” the Englishman said in a July 2018 Instagram Story. We’re both content and will continue to be friends.

In a similar vein, Sartorius announced, “The decision w mills were fully mutual & we are both delighted & continuing friends.”
