Erich Bergen Net Worth

Erich Bergen:

Erich Bergen is an American actor. He had his career beginnings in the late 2000s, making his debut as Paul Hoffman in two episodes of Gossip Girl. He next acted in the made-for-television movie Joey Dakota. In 2012, he had guest appearances in such television shows as Person of Interest, Franklin & Bash, and Childrens Hospital. Following this, he made his film debut in How Sweet It Is. He later portrayed Bob Gaudio in the 2014 movie Jersey Boys. At the same time, he landed his first starring role on television – as Blake Moran in Madam Secretary. His latest work in the movies was in 2017’s Humor Me.
