Where is Eline Powell from?- Childhood and Family

Eline Powell is a professional actress who was born in Leuven, Belgium. Her real name is Eline Pauwels, and she is the daughter of Rudi Pauwels, a Belgian pharmacologist, and Carine Claeys, a clinical researcher. Her parents were co-founders of the biotech company called Tibotec.

Since her parents travelled a lot, Powell also stayed in various cities and countries. Eline lived in Mechelen until she was fourteen and then moved to Switzerland before relocating to England.

Likewise, Powell is Belgian by nationality, while her ethnicity happens to be Caucasian. As for her education, young Eline enrolled at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Arts. She graduated in 2011 with a BA degree in acting.

Acting Career- Movies and TV Shows

Ever since she was a child, Eline Powell had wanted to be an actress. To pursue her acting career, young Powell even enrolled at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Arts and acquired her degree in acting. Eline commenced her career from the short student movie, For Elsie where she performed as Mila, a mobster’s daughter who wanted to learn piano in a day.

Her debut work earned her the Beijing Student Film Festival and the Student Academy Awards in the states. It was so impressive that even the film’s director, David Winstone, got the Foreign Film Gold Medal at the 39th Annual Student Academy Awards back in 2012.

Likewise, that same year Eline had a small role in Dustin Hoffman’s directorial debut, Quartet. After that, she appeared in an Italian drama film titled Anita B. directed by Roberto Faenza. Hollywood International Film Festival even gave the year’s Breakout Actress award.

Sadly, at the same time, The Hollywood Repoter criticized her performance as “ineffective…failing to convey any traces of the suffering her character has obviously endured.”

Despite that Powell continued her work and in August 2016, returned to small screen with the Freeform series titled Siren. Similarly, Eline then starred in two episodes of the much-successful HBO fantasy series, Game of Thrones as Bianca. Following this, she was seen in small roles in Novitiate and King Arthur: Legend of the Sword.
