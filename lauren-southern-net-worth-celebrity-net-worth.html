What is Lauren Southern's Net Worth?

Lauren Southern is a Canadian political activist, internet celebrity, YouTube personality, writer, and documentary film director who has a net worth of $500 thousand. Lauren Southern was born in Surrey, British Columbia, Canada in June 1995. She is described as alt-right although she denies it. She ran as a Libertarian Party candidate in 2015 for the Canadian federal election. Southern worked for The Rebel Media and has also worked for Spiked, The Libertarian Republic, and the International Business Times.

In 2017 she was detained by the Italian Coast Guard for blocking a ship that was embarking on a search-and-rescue mission while supported the white identitarian group Defend Europe. She was questioned under the UK Terrorism Act in 2018 and was denied entry to Britain. Lauren Southern embarked on a speaking tour of Australia in 2018 and attempted to speak in New Zealand although she was blocked. Her self-titled YouTube channel was created in 2015 and has more than 600 thousand subscribers and 50 million total views.
