All about Katheryn Winnick. What is her net worth as of 2023?

Who is the star behind the hit TV show ‘Vikings’ and what life experiences led her to her current career path? Read on to find out.

Table of Contents

Early Life

Originally of Ukrainian descent, Katheryn Winnick (born Katerena Anna Vinitska) was born in Ontario, Canada and did not learn English until she was 8 years old. Very passionate about martial arts, she earned her first black belt by age 13.

By age 21, she was teaching Taekwondo at her own school and it was there where she was inspired to enter the film industry by teaching actors to fight on movie sets.

Despite owning and teaching at three different Taekwondo schools, Winnick was still able to complete her degree in Kinesiology at York University in Toronto.

Film Career

Beginning in 2013, Winnick played Lagertha (and Directed) in the award winning hit TV show ‘Vikings’ which aired for 6 seasons and 76 episodes over the span of almost 10 years! The History Channel had initially meant the show to be a miniseries, but decided to expand the show from 10 episodes per season to almost 20 due to its popularity which became quite taxing on the showrunner.

Awards

She is the winner of ‘Best Actress’ in the short film ‘Daddy’s Little Girl (2002), ‘Best Female Performance’ in the short drama ‘Children of the Air’ (2012), and the winner of ‘Outstanding Film/Show Directed by a Woman’ for ‘Vikings’ in 2020.

In fact, eight of her nine nominations were due to the hit TV show, ‘Vikings.’

Interesting Facts


Winnick was once kicked out of summer camp at the age of 14 for reckless behavior.

Her last name ‘Winnick’ means ‘Winemaker.’

She speaks five languages: English, Ukrainian, French, Russian, and Italian.

Rumored to have been in a relationship with filmmaker Nick Loeb but neither ever confirmed the relationship.

Her mother, Lesya Winnick, is the Vice President of the Ukrainian Canadian Congress.

Winnick represents the luxury Swiss watch brand and named ambassador of Raymond Weil.

She is a third degree black belt in Tae Kwon Do, a 2nd degree black belt in karate, and a licensed bodyguard.
